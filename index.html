What is a Cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a decentralised digital currency, based on blockchain technology. There are about 5000 cryptocurrencies.


A cryptocurrency blockchain is an open distributed ledger that records various transactions in code. Transactions are recoreded in blocks, which are linked through a chain. Mining is when new units of cryptocurrency are released into the world, generally in exchange of validating transactions. The more the network grown, the more power is required to mine the crypto
